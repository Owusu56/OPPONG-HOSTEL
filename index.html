Looking to save more on your hostel rent?

Discover Opong hostel at Appiadu, Adom junction off the Donyina road and rent a hostel room select from two(2) in a room and three(3) in a room.


Study and Meeting Hall

A meeting room available on our premises where conferences,
meetings and other corporate events can be held.
The room is serene and conducive for studies but not restricted to
a particular academic discipline or discipline group.


2 in a Room

We have rooms for two on our facility.
These rooms have a washroom inside
but shares a common kitchen.


3 in a Room

There are rooms on our facility where 3 people are
meant to occupy. These rooms have washrooms in them
as well as a kitchen in each of these rooms.


Spacious Kitchen

We have a spacious kitchen where students or guests
can store, prepare and cook food and to
complete related tasks such as dishwashing.


Secure Parking

Convinient and secured parking facilities
are available for guests, ensuring peace
of mind for those traveling by car.
